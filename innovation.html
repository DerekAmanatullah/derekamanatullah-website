Zooly Labs

Founded 2025 · zoolylabs.com
Physical AI Disposable Robotics Sensor-Mediated ORS 2026 Business Innovation
“Physical AI that optimizes joint surgery through sensor-mediated disposable robotics, at a fraction of the cost.”

Robotic arms cost millions, sit idle between cases, and lock hospitals into single-vendor ecosystems. Zooly replaces them with disposable sensors that determine motion, impingement, and component orientation in real time, making precision surgery accessible to every operating room at a fraction of the cost.

Knimble Designs

Founded 2022
Smart Instruments Knee Arthroplasty Automatic Balancing
“Simplify knee replacement with automatic balancing, linked cutting, and dwell point prediction, without the cost and complexity of robotics.”

Achieving a balanced knee in surgery does not require an expensive robotic platform that most operating rooms cannot even afford. Knimble Designs solves this with smart instruments that automatically balance the flexion gap, link cuts, and predict the dwell point, giving every surgeon a kinematically superior knee, in less steps, and without the robot.

nSight Surgical

Founded 2020 · StartX 2021 · nsightsurgical.com
AI & Computer Vision Operating Room Safety SXSW Top 5 AI HLTH Surgical Transformation
“Like your keys falling out of your pocket, it is easier to find them right then, rather than going back an hour later.”

Surgical teams lose sponges, instruments, and needles inside patients, and contamination events go unnoticed until infection sets in. nSight Surgical uses AI and computer vision to watch the surgery and, in real time, catch what human memory misses before the wound is closed.

PlantarTech

Founded 2016 · plantartech.com
Dynamic Therapy Patient Compliance Plantar Fasciitis

Night splints for plantar fasciitis are bulky, uncomfortable, and end up in the closet. PlantarTech replaces static bracing with dynamic stretching that patients actually wear, because a device that sits unused treats nothing.
